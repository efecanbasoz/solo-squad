description: "Prepare a weekly client sync: progress, blockers, decisions needed, and next week's plan."
---

Prepare materials for a weekly client sync meeting.

1. **Progress update**: What was completed this week, with links to deliverables
2. **Metrics snapshot**: Key KPIs with week-over-week trend
3. **Blockers**: What's waiting on client input or decisions
4. **Decisions needed**: Specific choices the client must make, with your recommendation
5. **Next week plan**: What the team will work on, with expected deliverables

Format as a meeting agenda the client can follow along with.
Time-box: 30 minutes total. Don't overprepare.
