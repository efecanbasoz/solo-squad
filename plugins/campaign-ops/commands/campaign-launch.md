description: "Launch checklist for a new marketing campaign across channels."
---

Pre-launch checklist for a new campaign:

1. **Strategy check**: Target audience defined? Messaging approved? Budget allocated?
2. **Creative review**: Ad copy written? Landing pages live? UTM parameters set?
3. **Tracking verification**: /tracking-audit on the landing page. Pixels firing? Events configured?
4. **Platform setup**: Campaigns built in ad platforms? Targeting configured? Bid strategy set?
5. **Content ready**: Blog posts scheduled? Social posts queued? Email sequences loaded?
6. **Go/no-go**: Present checklist status. Get explicit approval before activating.

Post-launch:
- Monitor first 24 hours for tracking issues
- Check spend pacing after 48 hours
- First optimization review at day 7
