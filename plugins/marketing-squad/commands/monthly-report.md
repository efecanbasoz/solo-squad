---

Compile a monthly marketing performance report.

Gather data on:
- Organic: traffic, rankings, new pages indexed, backlinks acquired
- Paid: spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, CPA/ROAS by platform
- Social: followers, engagement rate, top posts, reach
- Content: pieces published, traffic driven, conversions attributed
- Email: sends, open rate, click rate, unsubscribes

Format:
- Month-over-month trends with directional arrows
- YTD performance vs goals
- Top 3 wins
- Top 3 concerns
- Recommended actions for next month

Keep it to 2-3 pages. Write for the client executive who has 5 minutes.
