description: "Prepare for a client call in 5 minutes: research, agenda, questions, and talking points."
---

Quick call prep. Ask the user: who's the call with, and what's the objective?

Then produce:
1. **2-minute research brief**: Company context, recent news, their role, previous interactions
2. **Agenda** (fit to the time slot): Opening, core discussion, next steps
3. **3 questions you must ask**: Tailored to the call objective
4. **Landmines to avoid**: Topics or framings that could derail the call
5. **Desired outcome**: The specific commitment you want by end of call

Keep the entire prep to one screen. This is a 5-minute exercise, not a strategy session.
