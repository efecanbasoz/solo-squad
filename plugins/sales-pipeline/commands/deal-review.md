---

Fast deal qualification. Ask the user about a specific deal, then score it.

For each MEDDPICC element, ask ONE targeted question:
- M: "Can the buyer put a dollar figure on the problem?"
- E: "Have you met the person who signs the contract?"
- D (criteria): "Do you know their evaluation criteria - written or unwritten?"
- D (process): "Can you draw the steps from today to signed deal?"
- P: "Have you mapped legal, procurement, and security review?"
- I: "What happens to them if they do nothing for 6 months?"
- C (competition): "Who else is in this deal, and why would they pick you over them?"
- C (champion): "Who inside is actively selling on your behalf?"

Score each 1-5. Total out of 40.
- 32+: Strong deal, invest heavily
- 24-31: Winnable but has gaps. Action plan needed.
- 16-23: At risk. Hard conversation about investment.
- <16: Not a deal yet. Re-qualify or walk away.
